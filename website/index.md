# fnayou/dotfiles

Personal, cross-platform dotfiles for **macOS** and **EndeavourOS / Arch Linux**, managed
with [GNU Stow](https://www.gnu.org/software/stow/) and themed with Catppuccin Macchiato.

This site documents what the dotfiles are, who they are for, the features they provide, and
how you can safely reuse parts of them on your own machine.
